Software Engineer

Think developing a video game was fun? Now launch it into orbit! As our Software Engineer, you'll architect the flight software that controls our student-built CubeSat in space. No prior space experience needed - just bring your software skills and desire to learn.

Embedded Software Engineer

Tired of Arduino projects? Want to see your code launch into space? As our Embedded Software Engineer, you'll program real satellite hardware and interface with sensors and components. Your code will control a real satellite in orbit! Bring your skills to our student CubeSat team.
